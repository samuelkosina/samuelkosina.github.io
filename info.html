I am a bachelor’s student of Architecture at Brno University of Technology, Faculty of Architecture, and a multidisciplinary artist working across architecture, installation, sound, and material experimentation. In my architectural projects, I focus on social topics, particularly the spatial design as a tool for social change. My approach is grounded in identifying structural or overlooked problems within society and translating them into architectural and artistic proposals. Alongside architectural design, I develop experimental projects involving sound, light, and DIY fabrication, often blurring the boundary between functional objects and artistic interventions. I work both independently and collaboratively on exhibitions, installations, and live projects.
